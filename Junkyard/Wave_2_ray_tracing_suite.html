💡 Why 4 pins?

P₁P₂ define the incident ray; P₃P₄ define the emergent ray. Removing the block and joining the lines reveals the path of light through the glass. The lateral shift between P₁P₂ and P₃P₄ shows the ray changed direction inside. Measuring i and r gives the refractive index n = sin i / sin r — a standard IGCSE practical.

💡 Why shallower?

Light from the coin goes water → air (slow → fast), bending away from the normal. Your brain assumes light travels in straight lines, so it projects the rays backwards — creating a virtual image above the coin's true position.

Apparent depth = Real depth ÷ n  ·  e.g. 15 cm in water: 15 / 1.33 = 11.3 cm
